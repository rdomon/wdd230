Tokyo Chamber of Commerce

OVERVIEW

PURPOSE

The Tokyo Chamber of Commerce actively listens to the opinions and requests of citizens, incorporating them into its organizational management. Additionally, we strive to revitalize the community by disseminating information about local specialties, tourism, and events.

SCENARIOS

  • What procedures are necessary to start a business in Tokyo?
  • What strategies can be implemented to resolve the labor shortage?
  • What local events will the tokyo chamber be holding this month?

TYPOGRAPHY

  • Heading Font: Sansita

  • Paragraph Font: Lato

Sansita font will be used for headings. Its distinctive design and high visibility attract readers and create an impression of sophistication.

The Lato font will be used for body text, making long texts easier to read and reducing visual fatigue.
